Platform

BioNeMo

NVIDIA BioNeMo is an AI platform for drug discovery that simplifies and accelerates the building and training of models using your own data, and scales deployment for drug discovery applications.

NVIDIA NIMs offer a set of optimized microservices for AI models used in drug discovery. These prebuilt containers provide state-of-the-art performance and can be deployed anywhere — going from zero to inference in minutes.
