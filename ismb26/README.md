# ISMB 2026 Workshop — NVIDIA

**Attendee Setup Guide**

Three quick steps: create a Brev account, add your workshop credits, and launch the hands-on labs. This takes about five minutes.

---

## Step 1 — Create your Brev account

Brev is the platform where you'll run every lab in this workshop. Open the Brev console and sign up (or sign in) with your email.

👉 **[Sign up at brev.nvidia.com](https://brev.nvidia.com/)**

---

## Step 2 — Add your workshop credits

Your labs run on GPU credits. Redeem the coupon code we provided so your account is funded before you launch anything.

1. In the Brev console, open the **Billing** tab in the top navigation.
2. Under **2. Credits**, click **Redeem Code**.
3. Enter the code from your workshop handout (format `ismb-2026-XXXXXX`) and click **Redeem**. Your balance updates immediately.

> **Tip:** Don't have the code? Ask a workshop facilitator — it's printed on your handout.

---

## Step 3 — Launch the labs

Once your credits are added, launch each lab directly from the links below. Each opens a ready-to-run environment in Brev.

| Lab | Title | Link |
|-----|-------|------|
| Lab 1 | Variant Effect Prediction | [Launch Lab 1](https://brev.nvidia.com/launchable/deploy/now?launchableID=env-3GKGRP6IGXtwtZKJcnTzcvbzgkx) |
| Lab 2 | BioNeMo ProteinaComplexa Binder Design | [Launch Lab 2](https://brev.nvidia.com/launchable/deploy/now?launchableID=env-3GFJZkBnuMJorl4nJ71j7oA5AdG) |

---

## Step 4 — Clean up!

> **Important:** When you're done, **delete your instances**. If you only stop them, they will **continue to accrue charges**.

In the Brev console, open each instance you launched and click **Delete** — not just Stop. Repeat for every lab environment you started.
