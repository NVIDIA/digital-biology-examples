# ISMB 2026 Schedule

**34th Annual Conference**  
Washington Hilton, Washington DC · July 12–16, 2026 · Hybrid In-Person & Virtual

---

## Tracks

| Track | Full Name | Description |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| 3DSIG | Structural Bioinformatics & Computational Biophysics | Structure-based drug discovery, structure prediction, docking, protein dynamics, macromolecular assemblies, molecular visualization, structural genomics. |
| BioInfo-Core | BioInfo-Core | Community for bioinformatics core facility managers and staff. Topics: core management, workflow development, reproducibility, best practices, training. |
| Bioinformatics in USA | Bioinformatics in the USA | One-day DC-special track showcasing breadth of US bioinformatics across health, biomedicine, biodiversity, and environmental/agricultural sciences. US-based researchers only. |
| BioVis | Biological Data Visualization | Visualization research for large, complex biological datasets. |
| BOKR | Bio-Ontologies & Knowledge Representation | 29th year at ISMB. Ontologies, knowledge graphs, knowledge-enhanced learning, FAIR data. Shares sessions with BOSC (Jul 14) and Function (Jul 13). |
| BOSC | Bioinformatics Open Source Conference | 27th annual BOSC. Open science, open source bioinformatics, standards, ontologies, open data, reproducibility, workflows. Keynotes: Eric Green & Maryam Zaringhalam. |
| CAMDA | Critical Assessment of Massive Data Analysis | 2026 Challenges: Health Privacy (bulk RNA-seq & scRNA-seq), Synthetic EHRs (diabetes), AMR Prediction, Gut Microbiome Health Index. |
| CompMS | Computational Mass Spectrometry | Proteomics, metabolomics, and lipidomics computational methods for mass spectrometry data. |
| CSI | Computational and Systems Immunology | ML, network, and immunogenomic approaches for immunology data. Single-cell multi-omics, spatial genomics, TCR/BCR repertoire profiling. |
| DREAM | DREAM Challenges | Crowd-sourcing complex biomedical research questions. 60+ challenges, 30,000+ participants. Includes DREAMQ — inaugural quantum DREAM challenge. |
| Education | Computational Biology Education | Bioinformatics education and training: curricula, competencies, teaching tools, and training resources. |
| Equity & Diversity | Equity and Diversity in Computational Biology Research | Research examining equity, representation, and diversity in datasets, methods, or the field. |
| EvolCompGen | Evolution & Comparative Genomics | Phylogenetics, multiple sequence alignment, comparative genomics, population genetics, cancer evolution. |
| Function | Gene and Protein Function Annotation | Computational and experimental approaches to gene and protein function prediction, including the CAFA challenge. |
| GenCompBio | General Computational Biology | Novel techniques in emerging areas not covered by specialized COSIs. |
| HiTSeq | High-throughput Sequencing | Genome assembly, haplotype phasing, transcriptomics, metagenomics, long reads, single-cell, pangenomes. |
| ICBO | International Conference on Biomedical Ontology | 17th ICBO — Theme: "Semantic Awareness in the Age of Generative AI". Jul 15–17, co-located. Includes VDOS Workshop (Jul 16). |
| iRNA | Integrative RNA Biology | Full range of RNA biology: splicing, polyadenylation, translation, non-coding RNA, RNA structure, modifications, long-read sequencing, RNA therapeutics. 4 keynote speakers. |
| MICROBIOME | MICROBIOME | Computational methods for microbiome research. Covers metaomics, microbial networks, pathogens, diagnostics. |
| MLCSB | Machine Learning in Computational & Systems Biology | Deep learning, graph inference, semi-supervised learning applied to genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, metabolomics. |
| NetBio | Network Biology | Novel methods for modeling and analyzing biological data as networks. Disease networks, pathways, systems-level analysis. |
| QLS ● | Quantum for Life Sciences | Quantum computing, hybrid quantum-classical, and quantum-inspired approaches for biology. Includes DREAMQ. |
| RegSys | Regulatory & Systems Genomics | Regulatory genomics: transcription factors, enhancers, epigenetics, alternative splicing, regulatory networks, single-cell, 3D genomics. |
| SysMod | Computational Modeling of Biological Systems | Mathematical modeling and bioinformatics for biological systems. Constraint-based, logical, kinetic, and multi-scale models. |
| Text Mining | Text Mining for Healthcare and Biology | Text mining tools and applications for biological/biomedical discovery. |
| TransMed | Translational Medical Informatics | Multi-omics + imaging + clinical data integration, precision medicine, biomarker discovery, personalized therapeutics. |
| VarI | Variant Interpretation | Genetic variant analysis: functional/structural impact prediction, GWAS, SNV prioritization, population genomics. |

> ● = Closed track (invite/registration required): Career Symposium, Industry Workshop, NIH/ODSS, NVIDIA Workshop, Pathogen Data Network Forum, Publishing Session, QLS, WEB.
